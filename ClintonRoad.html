About Clinton Road

Clinton Road is an eerie 10 mile stretch of road located in West Milford, Passaic County, New Jersey.

This curvy, creepy road is known for paranormal occurrences such as sightings of ghosts and strange creatures, gathering of witches, Satanists, and the Ku Klux Klan. There have also been rumors that murders dispose of bodies in the surrounding woods.

Come drive down Clinton Road for a chance to cross paths with a pack of blood-thirsty cannibals lurking in the shadows, hoping you'll stop your car to investigate.

Things to do in the Area

  • If you're brave, there are a few smaller roads that are connected to Clinton Road and allow you to park and explore a bit.
  • If you'd like to venture farther into the woods, you'll need an official hiking permit.
  • It is rumored that if you toss a coin off the "Ghost Boy Bridge," the ghost of a boy who drowned there will throw them back to you, or place them in the middle of the road the next time you drive down Clinton Road.
  • Take a night time drive down the road for a chance to witness some possible paranormal activities.
  • If you are interested in the history aspect of Clinton Road, there are roadside plaques that give you more information on the Ironworks that were abandoned in the mid-1800s.
